Rules & Help

Rules

This game is turn based
The rules are simple and are as follow :

  1. Place your ships
  2. Enemy places ships
  3. Shoot an enemy tile
  4. Enemy shoots a tile
  5. Repeat shooting until someone loses

Help

We created some new rules for this game :

  • Each ship has a Special Shot you can use during gameplay
  • the Special Shot cooldown is shared between all Special Shots
  • There are bonuses and maluses during gameplay
  • Bonuses can be used once
  • Maluses are always active
  • Special Shots, Bonuses and Maluses can be disabled in the OPTIONS
